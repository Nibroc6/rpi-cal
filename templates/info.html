Instructions for Uploading/Editing Events

If you don't already have one, you should get a personal upload link by sending me an email at larsec2@rpi.edu with the subject "Calendar Upload Link" and your name. At least in theory, this system should make it easier to track who uploads what.

After you get an upload link, you're ready to go. To create a new event (or events), first search at least one of the dates listed on the poster to make sure it hasn’t already been uploaded. Then, follow your link and upload a picture of a poster. The poster should mostly fill the picture, and should be relatively well lit. Any events on the poster will be automatically created, and you will be redirected to a page with edit links for the events. Make sure to save them somewhere, you won't get these again. At this point, if you feel particularly careful, you can take a moment to double-check that the events are correct, although in most cases it's unnecessary.


Event/Poster Guidelines

Any events that don’t follow these guidelines will be deleted, and, depending on the offense, you may have your upload privileges revoked.

  • Use common sense.
  • No personal events. Club meetings are fine, just don't put in ads for your personal party or anything like that.
  • Nothing explicit or offensive. If you wouldn't put it up outside DCC, don't put it on here.
